Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta (serves 4)

Ingredients

  • 12 oz (340 g) short pasta
  • 1 (28 oz / 800 g) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan

Directions

  1. Boil pasta until al dente; reserve 1/2 cup cooking water and drain.
  2. Simmer crushed tomatoes in a wide pan for 5 minutes to thicken.
  3. Stir in cream; cook 2 minutes until silky.
  4. Toss in pasta and basil, loosening with reserved water as needed.
  5. Off heat, fold in Parmesan and serve.
