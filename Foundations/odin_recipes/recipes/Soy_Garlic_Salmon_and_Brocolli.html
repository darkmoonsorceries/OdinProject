Honey–Soy Garlic Salmon & Broccoli (serves 4)

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (about 1 lb / 450 g total)
  • 12 oz (340 g) broccoli florets
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 200°C/400°F and line a sheet pan.
  2. Whisk soy sauce, honey, and garlic.
  3. Arrange salmon and broccoli on the pan; spoon half the sauce over everything.
  4. Roast 10–12 minutes, brushing salmon with remaining sauce halfway.
  5. Broil 1–2 minutes for a little char, then serve.
