Pesto Caprese Chicken (serves 4)

Ingredients

  • 4 small chicken breasts
  • 1/3 cup basil pesto
  • 8 oz (225 g) fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 Tbsp balsamic glaze

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 220°C/425°F; lightly oil a baking dish using the oil from the pesto.
  2. Coat chicken all over with pesto and arrange in the dish.
  3. Bake 15 minutes, then top with mozzarella and tomatoes.
  4. Return to oven 5–8 minutes until chicken is cooked through (74°C/165°F).
  5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and rest 5 minutes before serving.
